Freestyle Drones

For over a year now I have been into 'racing drones', also called Mini Quads or Mini Quadcopters. I don't do much racing though, I am into freestyle. I use all the same technology in racing drones, but I stick a GoPro on the front and record my flights stringing together flips loops and dives striving to be artistic and smooth (key word is striving). You can find my YouTube channel Sp00f FPV here. I've just built my first full sized racing drone at the begining of this year, I tought myself the basics of CAD and designed the frame myself and had it cut by a friend, designing the entire thing from the ground up. You can see a short article I wrote about that build here. That frame had some issues with the tollerences though from a combination of a budget cut and poor design, so I have rebuilt it onto a nicer frame.
